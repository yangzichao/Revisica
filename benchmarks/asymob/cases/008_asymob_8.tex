\begin{theorem}
Evaluate
$ \lim_{x \to \infty} \left(x - x^2 \cdot \ln\left(1 + \frac{ 1 }{ x }\right)\right) $
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{1}{2}
\]
\end{proof}
