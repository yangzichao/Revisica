\begin{theorem}
What is the integral of -3 + atan(x) + ln(tanh(3))
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
x \arctan(x) - \frac{1}{2} \ln(1 + x^2) + x \ln(\tanh(3)) - 3x + C
\]
\end{proof}
