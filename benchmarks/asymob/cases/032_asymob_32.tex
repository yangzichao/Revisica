\begin{theorem}
Consider the differential equation $\frac{dy}{dx} = xy$. Find the value of $y(\sqrt{2})$ given that $y(0) = 2$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
2e
\]
\end{proof}
