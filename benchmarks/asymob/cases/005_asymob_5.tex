\begin{theorem}
Compute $\lim_{x \to 0}\left(\frac{ 2 \cdot \cos(x)+4 }{ 5 \cdot x^3 \cdot \sin(x) }-\frac{ 6 }{ 5 \cdot x^4 }\right)$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{1}{150}
\]
\end{proof}
