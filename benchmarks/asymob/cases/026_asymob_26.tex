\begin{theorem}
Evaluate the indefinite integral. $\int \tan^{3}\!\left(x\right)\sec^{9}\!\left(x\right)   dx$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{\sec^{11}{\left(x \right)}}{11} - \frac{\sec^{9}{\left(x \right)}}{9}
\]
\end{proof}
