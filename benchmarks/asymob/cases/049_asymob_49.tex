\begin{theorem}
What is the integral of e^{x \left(x + 4\right)^{2}} \left(x + 4\right) \left(3 x + 4\right)
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
e^{x (x+4)^2}
\]
\end{proof}
