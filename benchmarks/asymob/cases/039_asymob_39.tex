\begin{theorem}
Evaluate the limit $\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1}\frac{1}{\sqrt{n^2-k^2}}$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{\pi}{2}
\]
\end{proof}
