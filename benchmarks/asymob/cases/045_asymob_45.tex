\begin{theorem}
What is the integral of 2x + 2x^2 + x[(x + x*e^x)^-1]
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{2 x^3}{3}+x^2-2 \tanh ^{-1}\left(2 e^x+1\right)
\]
\end{proof}
