\begin{theorem}
Evaluate the series $\sum\limits_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{2n+1}\left(\frac12\right)^{2n+1}$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\ln\sqrt{3}
\]
\end{proof}
