\begin{theorem}
Compute the first 4 nonzero terms of the Maclaurin series of $f(x) = e^x \cdot \cos(x)$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
1+x-\frac{x^3}{3}-\frac{x^4}{6}
\]
\end{proof}
