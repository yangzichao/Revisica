\begin{theorem}
Find the limit. $ \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{1-\cos\!\left(10x\right)}{\cos^{2}\!\left(6x\right)-1}$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{-25}{18}
\]
\end{proof}
