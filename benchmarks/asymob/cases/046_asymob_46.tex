\begin{theorem}
What is the integral of -x + cos[ln(sin(3))] * ln(3x)
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
-\frac{1}{2} x (x-2 \log (3 x) \cos (\log (\sin (3)))+2 \cos (\log (\sin (3))))
\]
\end{proof}
