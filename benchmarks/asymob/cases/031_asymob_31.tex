\begin{theorem}
Solve the following first-order differential equation:
$
\frac{dy}{dx} + 2y = e^{-x}, \quad y(0) = 1.
$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
e^{-x}
\]
\end{proof}
