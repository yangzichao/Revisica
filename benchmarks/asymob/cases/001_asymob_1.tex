\begin{theorem}
Compute the first 5 nonzero terms (not necessarily a quadratic polynomial) of the Maclaurin series of $ f(x) = e^{\sin(x)} $
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
1+x+\frac{x^2}{2}-\frac{x^4}{8}-\frac{x^5}{15}+\cdots
\]
\end{proof}
