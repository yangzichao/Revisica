\begin{theorem}
Evaluate the iterated integral $\displaystyle{\int_0^1dy\int_y^1(e^{-x^2}+e^x)dx}$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{3}{2}-\frac12 e^{-1}
\]
\end{proof}
