\begin{theorem}
Evaluate
$ \lim_{x \to 5} \left( \frac{ 3 \cdot x }{ x-5 }-\frac{ 3 }{ \ln\left(\frac{ x }{ 5 }\right) } \right) $
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{3}{2}
\]
\end{proof}
