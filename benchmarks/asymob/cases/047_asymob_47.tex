\begin{theorem}
What is the integral of 3x - 4*[cos(x+3)]*x^2
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The answer is:
\[
\frac{3 x^2}{2}-4 \left(x^2-2\right) \sin (x+3)-8 x \cos (x+3)
\]
\end{proof}
