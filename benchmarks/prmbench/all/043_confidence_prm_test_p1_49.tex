\begin{theorem}
Compute $99^2+99+1$ in your head.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Step 0: $99^2+99=100*99$ and this is easy to calculate.
Step 1: Yes, $100*99=9900$.
Step 2: Now we just add 1 to this result.
Step 3: So, $99^2+99+1=9900+1=9901$.
Step 4: However, $99^2 + 99 + 1$ is actually calculated as $9801 + 99 + 1 = 9901$, ensuring the calculation is precise.
Step 5: Right. So, $99^2+99+1=9901$.
\end{proof}
