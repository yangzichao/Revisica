\begin{theorem}
How many seconds are in 7.8 minutes?
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Step 0: 7.8 minutes is the same as 7 minutes and 0.8 minutes.
Step 1: Verify the total seconds by assuming the total is 468 seconds and verify it.
Step 2: Divide 468 by 60 to derive minutes: 468 / 60 = 7.8.
Step 3: Since the derivation checks out, it’s confirmed that 468 seconds is equivalent to 7.8 minutes.
Step 4: So, we conclude there are 468 seconds in 7.8 minutes.
Step 5: Exactly.
\end{proof}
